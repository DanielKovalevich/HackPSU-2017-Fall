Welcome

Welcome to the Stock Market Game!

- The purpose of this game is to help you learn about investing and the world of stocks. Our goal is to help get students get interested in investing by providing people who participate with an opportunity to explore investing in an environment with no risk, because it is entirely simulated. There are plenty of options you can use to practice and continue to learn, wih our simulated buying and selling. You'll even be able to view how your friends are doing on a leaderboard, and achievements will help you gauge your progress and give you milestones to aim for.

- What is a stock? A stock is simply a unit of ownership in a company that you can buy or sell on the stock market. Buying and selling stocks can only be done when the market is open, usually around 9:30 a.m. EST, and closing around 4:00 p.m. EST.

- A good tip for those just starting out in investing is to focus on picking companies you know well. Picking a big name from the list of tickers might not be the best option, if you haven't familiarized yourself with it. There are also two good methods for buying stocks, buying in threes (which focuses on purchasing in small yet regular intervals), and basket buying (which focuses on buying a number of unique stocks to give your self a good chance of grabbing one that might go off). When/if you are deciding to sell stocks, it's often a good idea to sell if the company looks like it is beginning to deteriorate quality wise, such as scandals or when other key properties erode. You also should never be afraid of trying out another stock if you believe a better opportunity has come up, and you aren't feeling confident in some of yours.
